📅 PART 2: CONTENT STRATEGY

What to Post (The 70-20-10 Rule)

  • 70%: Industry insights, project showcases, tips
  • 20%: Curated content (share others’ posts with your take)
  • 10%: Personal/behind-the-scenes

Post Ideas (30-Day Starter Pack)

Week 1: - Day 1: Introduce yourself + what 4th Wall Digital does - Day 3: Share a before/after of a 3D asset you created - Day 5: “3 things I’ve learned about AI-generated content for live events” - Day 7: Reshare an industry article with your commentary

Week 2: - Day 9: Behind-the-scenes of your workflow - Day 11: Client success story (Light Park case study) - Day 13: “The future of AI in event production” - Day 15: Friday tip: Best AI tools for 3D asset creation

Week 3: - Day 17: Showcase a recent project (with client permission) - Day 19: Poll: “What’s the biggest challenge in LED production?” + engage with responses - Day 21: Comment on industry trend (AI, LED tech, event innovation) - Day 23: Share a lesson learned from a project

Week 4: - Day 25: “How we created [specific asset] using AI + [tool name]” - Day 27: Client testimonial or tag a happy client - Day 29: Recap your month + announce what’s next - Day 30: Ask for referrals: “If you know anyone planning [type of event], I’d love to connect”

Posting Best Practices

  • Frequency: 3-5x per week
  • Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-2pm (when decision-makers browse)
  • Format: Text + image/video performs best
  • Hook: First 2 lines must grab attention (preview shows ~140 chars)
  • CTA: Always end with a question or call-to-action
  • Hashtags: 3-5 relevant ones (#LEDProduction #AI3D #EventTech #ExperientialMarketing #CreativeTech)

Engagement Strategy

  • Daily (10 min):
    • Comment on 5-10 posts from your network
    • Reply to all comments on your posts within 1 hour
    • Send 2-3 connection requests with personal notes

🤝 PART 3: CONNECTION BUILDING

Who to Connect With

  1. Event producers (your potential clients)
  2. LED technicians/designers (collaboration partners)
  3. Marketing agencies (referral sources)
  4. Festival/venue operators (direct buyers)
  5. Creative directors (peers + collaborators)
  6. AI/3D content creators (community building)

Where to Find Them

  • Search: “Event Producer” + filter by location/industry
  • Check who’s engaging with your posts
  • Look at connections of your current network
  • Join LinkedIn Groups: LED Professionals, Event Tech, AI Creators
  • Follow companies you want to work with → connect with their team

Connection Request Template

Hi [Name],

I saw your work on [specific project/post] and really admired [specific thing].

I create AI-generated 3D assets for LED productions and live events—recently worked with The Light Park on an AR game reaching 139K visitors.

Would love to connect and learn more about what you're working on!

Best,
Joe

DO NOT: - Send generic “I’d like to add you” requests - Immediately pitch your services - Connect without a reason

DO: - Personalize every request (mention their work) - Offer value first (insight, resource, introduction) - Follow up after they accept with a genuine conversation
